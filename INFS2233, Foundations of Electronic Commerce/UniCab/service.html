Our Services

Unicab offers a simple and inexpensive venue for students to organise their own carpooling service or ride with other customers.

What Unicab Offers

Our service simply provides a place for students to organise their own trips to and from university with other students, while establishing a user-curated carpooling community online.

It's Simple!

Using the Unicab website or our integrated mobile app, users simply select whether they're looking for a ride or offering to give one, state when and where they want to go, find each other on our integrated GPS, and go!

It's free!

With no add banners or monthly fees, Unicab is a completely costless service for all people in all walks of life. Avoid costly cabs and time-consuming public transport while meeting new people with a common goal.

Earn Money Driving for Students

With an optional cab fare system, you can pay an amount you choose to drivers who deserve compensation for your efforts. But don't worry, you can take a free ride or pay deeply, it's up to you!
