About Us

Unicab provides the cheapest method transport for students commuting to and from student venues and events, while creating a better commuting experience and fostering a social environment for travelling students.

Overview

Unicab is a simple, private service that provides students with the means to organise ridesharing amongst students. Operating soley based off comission on optional driver fares contributed by passengers, we endevour to facilitate the cheapest, most efficient and most environmentally conservative form of travel for students.

Our History

Opening in 2015, Unicab is a small Brisbane-centered partnership between 3 students with a commitment to providing better transport for students in the local area.
