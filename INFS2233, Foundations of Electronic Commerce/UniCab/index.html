Why choose UniCab

Unicab is an easy to use ride-sharing platform designed specifically for students within particular university networks. There are heaps of advantages to using Unicab for both drivers and passengers.

  • Allows drivers to easily share fuel and parking costs with their peers.
  • Enables passengers to travel to and from university while benefiting from the comfort of private transport.
  • Offers a financially feasible alternative to public transport methods such as bus, rail and ferry services.
  • Allows users to integrate with like-minded people within their university.
  • Easy to use.
  • Reduces both traffic and parking congestion both in and around university campuses.
